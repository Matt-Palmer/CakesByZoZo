Hi and welcome to my site!

Cakes by ZoZo is owned by me, Zoe Jackson – a self taught cake decorator from Luton, specialising in celebration cakes for all occasions, from birthdays, christenings and retirements, to hen parties and weddings.
If there’s something you’ve seen that you like, please do get in touch. But if you can’t see it in my portfolio of cakes, then it doesn’t mean I can’t make it for you.
